About
Solasta is the technical festival of the INDIAN INSTITUTE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY, DESIGN AND MANUFACTURING, KURNOOL (IIITDM), India. The word ‘Solasta’ means luminous or shining. The festival is held over for 3ays and two nights from 3rd to 6th January. It has so far seen twenty-four editions, having started in its current avatar in the year 2000. Solasta is entirely student-managed and is the first such event in the world to be ISO 9001:2015 certified.
